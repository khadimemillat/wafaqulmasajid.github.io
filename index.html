Welcome to Wafaqul Masajid

The Khadim-e-Millat Welfare Foundation manages a vital section of the organization that focuses on mosques and its management systems. This section is responsible for ensuring that mosques and all other religious sectors related to them are well-maintained and efficiently operated.

One of the main objectives of this section is to provide assistance to mosque committees in managing their affairs. This includes providing guidance on financial matters, organizing events, and ensuring that the mosque is clean and presentable at all times. The section also works to ensure that the mosque is accessible to all members of the community, regardless of their background or social status.

In addition to managing the day-to-day operations of the mosque, the Khadim-e-Millat Welfare Foundation's section for mosque management also works to organize educational programs and events. These programs aim to promote religious understanding and to encourage people to take an active role in their local mosque and the wider community.

The section also provides support for the upkeep and maintenance of the mosque building and its facilities. This includes repairs and renovations, as well as ensuring that the mosque is equipped with the necessary amenities to serve its members.

Moreover, the Khadim-e-Millat Welfare Foundation's mosque management section plays a crucial role in promoting social cohesion and community spirit. By organizing events and activities that bring together members of the community, the section helps to foster a sense of belonging and encourage people to work together for the greater good.

Overall, the Khadim-e-Millat Welfare Foundation's mosque management section is a vital component of the organization's efforts to promote social justice and community development. Through its work, the section helps to ensure that mosques remain an essential part of the community and that they continue to play a central role in promoting religious understanding and social cohesion.
